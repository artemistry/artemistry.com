Leaving This World More Beautiful Than You Found It

How do you create a picture that is meaningful?

First, ask yourself what is meaningful to you as an artist? Leaving this world more beautiful than you found it. Beauty can make the struggles that we’re dealing with everyday more tolerable. What’s beautiful to you? That’s a tough question and to answer it you need to be radically honest with yourself and that’s not easy.

But what’s the alternative? Wasted time and regrets? No, thank you. So do the work of being honest with yourself, building your skills to be yourself and building your courage to stay yourself when the situation is challenging. And with that, go give everything that you have to the world around you, as the opportunity to give is limitless and the demand for beauty is infinite.
