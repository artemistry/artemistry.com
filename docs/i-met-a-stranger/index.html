I Met A Stranger

I was doing my Confidence photoshoot in London and Mia was passing by. She saw us doing our photoshoot and she approached us to say hello. Mia was very friendly, full of energy and I invited her to be my next model. Within twenty minutes we've created this stunning expressive portrait.
