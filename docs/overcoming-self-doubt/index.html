Overcoming Self-Doubt

"Confidence, a word that is often used in my life that I need more of. I have 3 little boys under the age of 5 and have always struggled with myself and my body. It is only as I am getting older that I have learned that it’s all in my head, I am starting to push through self-limiting beliefs, no longer will I stop myself from becoming who I want to be, I can do it. I am raising my curiosity level and overcoming self-doubt, I am now pushing myself from my comfort zone and facing my fears. I am starting to believe in myself, believing I have a future and I can be who I want to be even as a stay at home mum. This photoshoot helped me, a lot... I pushed through boundaries I thought I never would, and I loved it - I felt sexy, empowered and on top of the world."
