Do It For You

I was listening to Jim Rohn’s record where he said something that was contrarian to the common wisdom. He said: "I will take care of me for you, if you will take care of you for me".

I often work with models who do photoshoots to take care of themselves. It’s an emotional care. It’s when you need to love yourself more. Photoshoots can give you that care. They raise your sense of self-esteem, self-confidence and self-love.

And they’re a way to have fun, when you’re bored of your daily routine.

And they’re a way to express yourself, when you can’t express enough going through days, weeks, months.

Whatever you do - take care of you for others. And ask others to take care of themselves for you.
